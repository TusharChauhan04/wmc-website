Our Services

Comprehensive healthcare solutions delivered by specialists.

Aesthetic Treatments

Aesthetic department focuses on helping clients achieve their beauty, confidence, and sophistication through advanced techniques such as facial and body rejuvenation procedures. They offer a wide variety of treatments, including Hydra facial, cosmetic treatments, laser hair removal and more.

The aesthetic specialists at WMC are highly qualified and have extensive experience in the area of aesthetics. Each patient is carefully assessed and a personalized, comprehensive treatment plan will be developed based on their individual needs to enhance and improve their look.

Laser Hair Reduction

Say goodbye to unwanted hair with our advanced laser hair removal technology. We use highly efficient and skin-safe systems suitable for all skin types, ensuring long-lasting smoothness with minimal discomfort.

Cosmetic Treatments

Boost your confidence with our range of cosmetic treatments performed by skilled professionals using top-grade products and advanced techniques.

Hydra Facial

Give your skin a deep, refreshing cleanse with our signature Hydra Facial treatments. This multi-step, medical-grade facial detoxifies, hydrates, and brightens the skin for an instant, radiant glow.

General Practitioner (GP)

General Practitioners (GPs) provide comprehensive, compassionate primary care for individuals and families, serving as the first point of contact for most health concerns. They evaluate symptoms, diagnose common illnesses, manage chronic diseases, and guide you through preventive care such as health screenings and vaccinations.

GPs at WMC coordinate your care across other medical specialties to ensure timely referrals and continuity of treatment when needed. Their focus is on long-term health, early detection of potential problems, and personalized care plans that prioritize your overall well-being.

Pediatrics

Pediatrics at WMC provides compassionate, family-centred care for infants, children, and teenagers, focusing on their physical, emotional, and developmental needs. The team works to ensure each child receives safe, age-appropriate care in a supportive and reassuring environment.

Our doctors are trained in all aspects of pediatric health, from routine check-ups, vaccinations, and developmental screening to the management of acute illnesses and long-term conditions. Care plans are tailored to each child and family, with an emphasis on prevention, early intervention, and ongoing health education.

Urology Services

Our Urology Department offers advanced diagnostic and therapeutic care for male reproductive and urinary system conditions. From urinary tract disorders and prostate diseases to kidney, bladder, and sexual health issues, our certified urologists provide both minimally invasive and complex surgical treatments.

With a person-centered, evidence-based approach, we focus on patient comfort, safety, and lasting wellness. Using the latest technology, our non-invasive treatments promote tissue regeneration, improve function, and restore confidence. The WMC team is committed to delivering modern, compassionate, and comprehensive urological care tailored to your needs.

Urology Consultations:

Meet with our experienced urologists for expert evaluation, diagnostic testing, and personalized treatment plans tailored to your specific condition.
